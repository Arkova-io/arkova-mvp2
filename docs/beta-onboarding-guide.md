# Arkova Closed Beta — Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the Arkova closed beta! This guide walks you through signing up, securing your first document, and verifying it.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to **[app.arkova.ai](https://app.arkova.ai)**
2. Click **"Create account"**
3. Enter your **invite code** (provided in your invitation email)
4. Choose how to sign up:
   - **Google:** Click "Continue with Google" to sign up with your Google account
   - **Email:** Fill in your name, email, and password, then click "Create account"

## Step 2: Choose Your Account Type

After signing up, you'll be asked to choose your account type:

- **Individual** — Personal document security. Secure your own credentials (degrees, certificates, licenses).
- **Organization** — Business document security. Issue and manage credentials for your team or clients.

> **Note:** If your email domain matches an existing organization (e.g., @arkova.ai), you'll be prompted to join that organization automatically.

## Step 3: Secure Your First Document

1. From your **Dashboard**, click **"Secure Document"**
2. Upload any document (PDF, image, or other file)
3. Arkova generates a unique cryptographic fingerprint **on your device** — your document never leaves your computer
4. Add metadata (title, type, description)
5. Click **"Secure"** to anchor the fingerprint to the network

> **Tip:** You can also use **Bulk Upload** to secure multiple documents at once using a CSV or XLSX file.

## Step 4: View Your Secured Record

Once your document is secured:
- A unique **verification link** is generated (e.g., `app.arkova.ai/verify/ARK-2026-001`)
- You can share this link with anyone who needs to verify your document
- A **QR code** is also generated for easy sharing
- Download a **PDF proof certificate** for your records

## Step 5: Verify a Document

Anyone can verify a document — no account needed:

1. Go to the **verification link** or scan the **QR code**
2. The verification page shows:
   - Document fingerprint
   - Issuer information
   - Timestamp of when it was secured
   - Network confirmation status
   - Explorer link to the on-chain proof

Or use the **Search page** at [app.arkova.ai/search](https://app.arkova.ai/search) to search by fingerprint or text.

---

## For Organizations

If you chose the **Organization** account type:

1. **Set up your organization** — Enter your company name and domain
2. **Invite team members** — Go to Organization → "Invite Member"
3. **Create credential templates** — Go to Settings → Credential Templates
4. **Issue credentials** — Upload documents or use bulk upload with CSV/XLSX
5. **Manage your team** — View member activity, change roles, revoke access
